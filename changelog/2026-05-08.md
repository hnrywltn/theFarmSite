## Google Drive Integration
- Server proxies Drive images through `/api/photos/:id` (avoids CORS/throttling)
- Photos listed newest-first (`createdTime desc`)
- Full Shared Drive support (`supportsAllDrives: true`) for upload quota
- Upload endpoint: `POST /api/photos/upload` — accepts multiple images via multer, writes to Drive folder

## Gallery — Upload
- **+ Add Photos** button top-right of gallery header
- Opens native file picker (multiple images, up to 20 at once)
- Uploads to Google Drive Shared Drive folder, gallery refreshes on completion
